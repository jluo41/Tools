🗺️  subjective-label Plugin — Map at a Glance
==============================================

  One researcher + one moderator + 8 worker agents + 5 personas.
  The researcher only ever talks to 🤖 moderator. Everything else
  is orchestrated through the Task tool.

  🧑 researcher

     |  /sl-scale          🎯 batch-label full corpus
     |  /sl-status         🔭 state · κ trajectory · gallery

─§ ⚡ Compact form ─────────────────────────────────────────

  🧑 → /sl-init → /sl-iterate × N → /sl-validate → /sl-scale
                       ▲                  │
                       └─ 🚩 STALLED ─────┘

  🤖 moderator orchestrates · 8 workers · 5 personas · 1 disk DAG.


─§ 📌 Three cross-cutting truths ───────────────────────────

  1. 🧑 researcher talks ONLY to 🤖 moderator. No exceptions.

  2. 📥 sampler is the single entry point for any "pick N items"
     decision — 5 modes (init_map, iterate_batch, validate_heldout,
     scale_preflight, diagnostic) share the same primitives.

  3. 📐 embedder = speed · 👥 panel = judgment · 🧠 classifier =
     middle ground. Cascade composes them in the SMALL loop.
