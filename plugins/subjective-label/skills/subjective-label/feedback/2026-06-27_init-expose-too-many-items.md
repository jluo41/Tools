Step 3 (Expose) dumps 15 reviews at once. Too much — I cannot express my own intent for what I want to label. The wall of text blocks the researcher from thinking.

Current behavior: Moderator shows 10-15 diverse items in a big table and asks "how would you label each?" The researcher is overwhelmed and can't articulate their dimension.

Desired behavior: Start slower. Let the researcher express their intent FIRST (what does agreeableness mean to them, what signals they expect), THEN show a few examples (3-5) to test that intent against real data. Iterate in small rounds, not one big dump.

The protocol says "DO NOT ask for label schema yet" but there's a middle ground: ask what the researcher is LOOKING FOR (signals, cues, what makes a doctor seem agreeable) without forcing them into formal labels. Then show data that tests those intuitions.
