Vitals reviews are 63% ratings-only (no text in the `Comments` field). Platform-level text availability varies dramatically across review platforms. Filter by text presence and minimum length before sampling, or the corpus will contain unlabelable items. In the physician review corpus: healthgrades/zocdoc/yelp/ratemds have text in most reviews; vitals is the outlier.
