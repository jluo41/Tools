─§ 🎚️ Two cross-cutting bands ──────────────────────────────

  🛠️  00_meta       — available at every phase
  🎯  13_venue      — informs phase 06+ and final submission

  Both are not "next-phase" steps; they shape how the linear
  walk is executed.


─§ 🔀 Dependencies are NOT strictly linear ─────────────────

  The numbers say  "X usually runs before Y".  But:

  • 09_figures is in the linear path AFTER 06-08 for reading
    convenience, but figure-planner (the structural one) runs
    in parallel with 05_prewrite. PRINCIPLES.md #2.

  • 10_review wraps around 06-08: paper-claim-audit runs
    continuously during writing; submission-audit gates only
    at the end.

  • 13_venue is consulted at 05_prewrite (article-type) AND at
    10_review (preflight) AND at 11_respond (rebuttal style).

  Treat the map as a sequence diagram, not a strict pipeline.
