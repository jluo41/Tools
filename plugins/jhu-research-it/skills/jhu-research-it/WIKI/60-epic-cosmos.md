# 🌌 Epic Cosmos

## What it is
Epic Cosmos is a virtual dataset built by a community of health systems that use Epic. Participating organizations combine their data to enable research and clinical insight. (Source: https://cosmos.epic.com/about/)

## Scale
More than 300 million patient records, including about 50 million pediatric records, from over 38,000 clinics and 1,600 hospitals across the United States. Inpatient and outpatient charts are integrated into one record as patients move between health systems. (Source: https://cosmos.epic.com/about/ , https://www.research.chop.edu/applications/epic-cosmos)

## What you can do with it
- Query de-identified EHR data at population scale.
- Run statistical analysis and predictive modeling on the cohort.
- Find patients who match clinical-trial criteria.
