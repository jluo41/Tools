# 🔐 SAFER — Secure Analytic Framework Environment for Research

## What it is
SAFER is the Research Virtual Desktop tier of the Secure Analytic Framework Environment. Together with SAFE Desktop it is meant to support researchers, enable collaboration with internal and external researchers, and speed up analysis. (Source: https://researchit.jhu.edu/safer/)

## SAFE vs SAFER
- **SAFE Desktop**: for relatively small and uncomplicated analysis projects. See `20-safe.md`.

## Storage
SAFESTOR is the large research data storage that mounts as the `R:` drive on both SAFE and SAFER desktops: up to 100 TB, first 10 TB free for 3 years. (Source: researchit.jhu.edu)
