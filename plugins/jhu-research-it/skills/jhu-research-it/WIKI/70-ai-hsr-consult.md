# 🧭 AI use in research — AI HSR Consult + governance

If your project develops AI, or uses an existing AI tool, in human-subjects research (HSR), get an AI HSR Consult before you build or deploy. This applies to using an external LLM inside SAFE, serving a model trained on REACH data, or any AI that touches study data.

## AI HSR Consult (JHM Office of Human Subjects Research)
- What it is: OHSR expanded its consult service to cover research teams that are developing AI or intend to use existing AI tools to support a study. (Source: ICTR announcement, link below.)
- When to use it: in the planning phase of the study. A team may also be referred for an AI consult by the IRB or an associated ancillary review committee.
- Who is on it: experts from the IRB and the Data Trust, plus faculty consultants across disciplines including medicine and engineering.
- How to request: complete a brief questionnaire; you are then contacted to schedule a virtual (MS Teams) meeting with OHSR staff. [TODO: JL paste the direct questionnaire / intake link]
