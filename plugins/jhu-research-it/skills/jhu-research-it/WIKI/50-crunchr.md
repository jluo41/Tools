# 🧪 Crunchr — collaborative data science on SAFE

## What it is
Crunchr is a collaborative data-science platform based on Jupyter. It ships with Python and R support inside JupyterLab and RStudio. For security, Crunchr can only be reached from inside the SAFE Desktop. (Source: search of researchit.jhu.edu, 2026-06-25)

- **Databricks**: Spark-scale data, model training, endpoint building (see `40-databricks.md`).
