# Stage 02: Seed Pitch

## What This Scene Does

Stage 02 writes the one-minute public story for the paper.

The pitch asks: if a reader gives us one minute, what should they understand and
remember?

## Problem It Solves

It turns a paper prospectus into a story gate. The pitch reveals whether the
current narrative and evidence can support a real paper, or whether the work
needs another project-side loop.

## Artifacts It Reads

Project-side:

- narrative current state
- discoveries and literature notes
- task/probe outputs
- insight cards

Paper-side:

- `lifecycle/stage00_topic-appears/current.md`
- `lifecycle/stage01_create-paper-folder/current.md`

## Files It Changes

The lifecycle artifact is:

```text
lifecycle/stage02_seed-pitch/
  current.md
  runs/
  feedback/
  assets/
```

In manuscript mode, the accepted pitch may also be mirrored to `0-pitch/`, but
the lifecycle branch remains the teaching and decision artifact.

## When It Moves Forward

Move to Stage 03 when the pitch is clear enough to ask for an evidence-backed
narrative:

- central question is understandable;
- tentative claim is narrow enough;
- evidence direction is visible;
- biggest fragility is explicit.

## When It Loops Back

Loop back to project narrative when:

- the pitch sounds good but evidence is weak;
- the story depends on a missing outcome definition;
- the clinical or empirical framing is unclear;
- the evidence exists but has not been synthesized into a stable narrative.

## Relation To Project Lifecycle

Stage 02 is a gate, not a draft. It either:

- motivates another narrative/project loop, or
- promotes the paper toward Stage 03 evidence-backed narrative.

The normal loop is:

```text
pitch -> narrative -> discover/probe/task -> insight -> narrative -> pitch
```
