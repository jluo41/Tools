# MS-IS Exemplars - content to imitate

Store the CONTENT of similar Management Science (IS department) papers here (PDF, or
extracted .md/.txt), so the writing stages can imitate their **language style and
preferences**. This is NOT a citation list (that is `../references/README.md`); it is
a style corpus.

## How to add

- Drop a PDF: `exemplars/<slug>.pdf` (e.g. `author-2022-mnsc-platform-pricing.pdf`).
- Or store extracted text: `exemplars/<slug>.md` (the structured abstract +
  contribution paragraph + a few Model/Results and Discussion paragraphs are enough
  to capture style).
- Prefer open-access or author-provided PDFs.

## What to capture per exemplar (feeds `../style-profile.md`)

- Structured-abstract arc and exact section phrasing.
- How the economic / analytical contribution is stated in one paragraph.
- Proposition/Theorem phrasing and how welfare is reported (analytical); or the
  identification-and-mechanism sentence pattern (empirical).
- Discussion structure (managerial/policy implications -> limitations -> conclusion).
- Tone, sentence length, notation discipline, how it positions against economics / OR
  / management-science literature.

## Candidate topics to source (similar to this paper)

economics-of-IS empirics or analytical models in the paper's domain (platform
economics, IT investment and value, digital markets, privacy/security economics,
sharing/gig platforms, AI/automation economics, FinTech, healthcare IT economics);
hybrid model-plus-empirics exemplars if relevant; foundational IT-economics papers
the manuscript builds on.
