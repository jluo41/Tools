In section outline files (e.g., 3-theory.md), the parenthetical lines at the top of each paragraph block are PREVIEWS of what the paragraph should contain. They are structural notes, not draft text. Never add citation keys (e.g., `[graziano1996perceiving]`) to these preview lines. Citations belong only in the actual draft sentences below the preview. The preview lines stay clean and readable as a structural guide.
