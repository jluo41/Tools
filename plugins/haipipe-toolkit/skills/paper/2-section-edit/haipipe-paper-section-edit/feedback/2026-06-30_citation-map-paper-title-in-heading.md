Each citation entry in _CITATION_*.md must include the full paper title in its ### heading (e.g., `### P1.S2 — Graziano, Jensen-Campbell, & Hair (1996). Perceiving interpersonal conflict and reacting to it: The case for agreeableness.`). Without the title, the user cannot tell what each citation IS at a glance. The heading should be: `### Pn.Sn — Authors (Year). Title.`
