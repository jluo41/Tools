# Audience Profile: Executive

## Tone
Direct, outcome-oriented. Minimal jargon. Focus on impact
and next steps.

## Constraints
- Length: ≤ 600 words
- Jargon: minimal; translate to business impact
- Reading level: professional/business
- Citations: endnote — "See knowledge entry K03."

## Lifecycle mappings

### → Claims
Claims translated to business metrics: ROI, cost savings,
patient retention, compliance risk reduction.

### → Design
Lead with the bottom line. One-paragraph summary first.
Then supporting evidence. End with ask/recommendation.

### → Variants
Executive summary (short) + optional detail appendix.

### → Draft
"So what?" test on every paragraph. No methodology details
unless requested. Charts preferred over tables.
