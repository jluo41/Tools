# Email Style Profile

Drafting guide for email artifacts.


## Voice examples

**Patient email (warm):**
```
Subject: Your [Medication] refill is coming up

Hi [Name],

We noticed your [Medication] prescription will need a
refill in about 2 days. Staying on schedule with your
medication helps keep your levels steady and reduces
the chance of a gap in coverage.

Here's what you can do:
• Call your pharmacy at [PharmacyPhone]
• Use this link to request a refill: [URL]
• Reply to this email if you need help

We're here to support you.

Best,
[ClinicName] Care Team
```

**Clinician email (clinical):**
```
Subject: Weekly refill risk summary — 4 patients flagged

Dr. [Name],

This week, 4 patients in your panel are at elevated risk
for refill lapse based on timing analysis (K03).

Key findings:
• 2 patients have < 48h until Rx expiry with no refill
• 1 patient has missed the last 2 refill windows
• 1 patient opted out of SMS reminders

Recommended actions:
• Review the patient list: [DashboardURL]
• Consider direct outreach for the 2 repeat-miss cases

— [SystemName] Insights
```


## Drafting rules

1. Subject line ≤ 60 chars, specific (not "Important Update").
2. 3-5 sections following narrative arc.
3. Bullet lists for actionable items.
4. Links use descriptive anchor text.


## Self-review checklist

```
[ ] Subject line ≤ 60 chars and specific
[ ] 200-800 words (per audience)
[ ] Sections follow narrative arc
[ ] Every claim cites K/W (format per audience)
[ ] cited_K / cited_W in frontmatter
```
