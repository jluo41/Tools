ID   B93/03 — PatchTST-style + FiLM(23-d patient features), MULTI-SEED (N=10)
📂 source         B93_event_arch/03_patchtst_plus_features/
                    03_patchtst_plus_features.py
                  (no config YAML — args via CLI)
📥 Data           EventGlucose / @v0004_welldoc_dem_events_tewindow
                  splits = 22,830 / 373 / 2,767 / 6,324  (train/val/test-id/test-od)
                  filter = drop Med5Min-triggered cases
🧱 Architecture   channel-indep patch transformer:
                  patch_len=24, stride=12, d_model=128, n_heads=4,
                  n_layers=3, dropout=0.1
                  3 channels (cgm, carbs, exer) + FiLM(23-d patient vec)
                  ~1-2M params
⚙️ Training       30 epochs cosine LR 5e-4, batch 128, AdamW, grad-clip 1.0
                  10 seeds {0..9}
🎯 Loss           MSE on cgm[287:311]
🔗 Recipe notes   train-from-scratch on EventGlucose (no pretrain)
📊 Results (mean ± std, N=10):
                  val      28.37 ± 0.18
                  test-id  29.57 ± 0.28
                  test-od  29.03 ± 0.26
📈 Paired-t vs same-arch baseline (no patient features):
                  val      Δ = -2.20  p = 0.0372  ✅
                  test-id  Δ = -2.39  p = 0.0492  ✅
                  test-od  Δ = -1.39  p = 0.1663  🟡
🔢 Sign test      10/10 seeds negative on val + test-id; 9/10 on test-od
💾 Saved          examples/.../03_patchtst_plus_features/results/multi_seed/
                  aggregated.json + comparison.md + seed_{0..9}/summary.json
⏱  Time           ~60 min total on 1× RTX 4090
📝 Notes          seed 4 baseline failed to converge (val_rmse stuck at 50);
                  outlier-excluded N=9 analysis gives Δ ≈ -1.2 / -1.0 / -0.5,
                  paired-t p<0.005 on val and test-id.
                  Side finding: with_patient std is 10-20× smaller than
                  baseline std — FiLM patient conditioning stabilizes training.
