# Venue Routing

## Default

If the venue is not explicitly given, assume a `Nature`-style journal manuscript rather than a conference paper.

## Flagship Nature

Use `Nature` only when:
- the contribution matters beyond the immediate specialty
- the story can be framed for broad scientific readership
- the paper does not depend on highly local field context to sound important

## Nature Methods

Prefer `Nature Methods` when:
- the central contribution is a method, assay, platform, computational framework, or enabling resource
- validation, benchmarking, reproducibility, and general utility are central to the story

## Nature Biotechnology

Prefer `Nature Biotechnology` when:
- the paper has clear biotechnology or translational significance
- platform utility, engineering depth, or real-world impact are central
- the manuscript can justify whether it is an `Article` or a `Resource`

## Conference Exception

Use `conference-paper-writing` only when the workflow is explicitly conference-first for venues such as:
- NeurIPS
- ICML
- ICLR
- ACL
- AAAI
- COLM
