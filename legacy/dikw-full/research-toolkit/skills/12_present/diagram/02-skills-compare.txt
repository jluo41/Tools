─§ Shared Principles ────────────────────────────────────────────────

✅ No fabrication — all numbers must come from source paper
✅ State persistence (JSON) — workflows are resumable mid-phase
✅ Multiple output formats — PDF primary + editable PPTX/SVG
✅ Venue-aware — color schemes adapt to NeurIPS / ICML / CVPR / ACL…
✅ De-AI polish — remove "delve", "pivotal", "leverage", "facilitate"
✅ Content ratio — 30–40% of paper content; distilled to key insights
