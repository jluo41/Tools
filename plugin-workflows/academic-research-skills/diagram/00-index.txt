─§ 🎯 Core idea ────────────────────────────────────────────

  A paper should not begin at "topic selected".

  It should begin when two lines converge:

      🧪 Probe line        →  facts · evidence · claim support
      🎭 Narrative line    →  angle · urgency · reader desire

  When they meet, the author does not feel pushed to write.
  The author feels eager to sell the story.

─§ 🧨 One-line memory ──────────────────────────────────────

  Research gives possibility.
  Probe gives truth pressure.
  Narrative gives desire.
  Only when truth and desire meet should writing begin.
